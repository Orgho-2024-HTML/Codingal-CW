Doraemon is a cat-shaped robot from the 22nd century who lives with his friend Nobita. He is a popular character in Japanese manga and anime. The series has focused on 5 main characters named Doraemon, Nobita, Shizuka, Gian, Suneo

  • Nobita - A guy who is not good in studies, sports but is G.O.A.Ted in shooting and sleeping

  • Shizuka - Nobita's good friend

  • Suneo - A rich guy, who always flexes in front of his friends

  • Gian - A son of a grocery item seller, his real name is 'Goda Takeshi'in actual anime
